About Me 个人简介

JIALUN CAO (曹嘉伦)

I am currently a research postgraduate student in CASTLE lab, pursuing my Ph.D degree under the supervision of Prof. S.C. Cheung at HKUST. My research interests include but not limited to software analysis and testing, mining code repositories. My current work focuses on testing, verifying and understanding Deep Neural Networks.
